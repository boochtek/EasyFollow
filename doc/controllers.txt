Controllers
===========
Home page
    Show Home Page (if not logged in)
    Show My Profile (if logged in)
Login page

Signup Step 2 (Add Networks)
    Show Signup Step 2 (composite page)
        List My Networks
        Add Network
        Remove Network
Signup Step 3 (Connect)
    Show Signup Step 3 - similar to Show My Profile?
Networks - used in Show Signup Step 2 and Show My Profile
    List My Networks (shows all networks we can add, and info on ones we've added)
    List Their Networks (shows info on networks they've added)
    Add Network (enter username, authenticate to the network)
    Remove Network
Connections
    List My Connections
    List Their Connections
    List My Connections Network (friends-of-friends)
    Add Connection (all networks, or single network)
    Remove Connection (all networks, or single network)

        List My News Items
        List My Connections Network

        Show Their Bio
        Add Connection (all networks)
        List Their Networks
        Add Connection (single network)
        List Their Connections (possibly filtered)
    Show Their Public Profile (if not logged in)
        Show Their Bio
        List Their Networks
        List Their Connections (possibly filtered)
